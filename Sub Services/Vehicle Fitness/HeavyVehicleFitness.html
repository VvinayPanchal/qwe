    Heavy Vehicle Fitness


    For every owner of a heavy vehicle, ensuring its fitness is crucial for a robust operation. Krishna-RTO is your dependable partner for streamlined and personalized Fitness Certificate services, ensuring your heavy vehicles keep moving strong and legally compliant.


    Eligibility:

    All goods vehicles exceeding 7,500 kg gross vehicle weight (GVW) registered in all over India and Your State require a valid FC for operation.

    Multi-axle vehicles must be under 15 years old, while non-multi-axle vehicles must be under 12 years old for obtaining a new FC.

    The vehicle must not have any pending challans or traffic violations.

    No major structural damage or engine malfunction should be present in the vehicle.

    Required Documents:

    1. Machine and machine bill

    2. Viper

    3. Indicator

    4. Brake Light

    5. TT Horn

    6. The bumper should not be fitted at the front in a small vehicle and should be fitted at the rear in a big vehicle.

    7. The glass should not be broken

    8. The car should be empty

    9. Radium 3 mm is used

    10. Chassis should not be big

    11. If the permit is valid and if the vehicle is from outside (like if the vehicle of another RTO has to be fitted in some other RTO)

    12. Fastag is installed

    13. Pollution card should be valid only for 6 months

    14. The body of the vehicle should not be damaged.

    15. The vehicle should have the correct number plate (HSRP) and the number should be written clearly.

    16. In the RC of the vehicle, open or close is written correctly according to the body.
